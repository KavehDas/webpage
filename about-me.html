About Me

My name is Kaveh Dastouri, and I was born in Heris, East Azerbaijan, Iran, in 1995. I completed my bachelor’s degree at Farhangian University, followed by a master’s degree in algebra at the University of Tabriz. I then earned my Ph.D. in mathematics at Tarbiat Modares University in Tehran under the supervision of Prof. Ali Iranmanesh. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tarbiat Modares University specializing in algebra, focusing on representation theory and finite groups, and I will soon begin a new postdoctoral position at the Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research (BICMR).

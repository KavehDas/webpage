Biography

I was born in Heris, East Azerbaijan, Iran, in 1995. I am a postdoctoral researcher specializing in algebra, focusing on representation theory and finite groups.

Research Interests

  • Representation Theory of Finite Groups: Including ordinary and modular cases.
  • Finite Groups and Their Applications: Especially in combinatorics.
  • Public Key Cryptosystem Scheme Design: Using hard computational representation problems.

Current Projects

  • Researching π-partial character degrees and their influence on the structure of groups.
  • Engaging with classical number theory problems, applying representation theory approaches.
  • Creating public key cryptosystems.
  • Creating a package for π-partial characters and π-structure of groups in GAP.
